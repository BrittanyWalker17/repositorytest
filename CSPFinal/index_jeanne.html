KOKO ROSE

JEANNE WALKER

____________________



Going by the name Koko Rose, Jeanne Walker is a self-taught musician, hailing from Rochester Minnesota, where she grew up with her twin sister, Brittany, and mother, Nancy. The now LA-based artist holds roots in singing and song writing since the tender age of 8. Jeanne obtained her music roots from her mother, a folk singer. Her music is best described as “dreamy, sad, alternative pop music.” Koko Rose's songs are the result of plentiful self-reflection, love, hurt, sadness, strength, and facing the unrequited. When Jeanne was 14 she began performing in restaurants, open mics, pubs, while occasionally busking. When she started getting noticed around her home-town and near by cities, she made her way to newspaper covers and blogs, which lead her to make the move to Chicago to study music, and eventually Los Angeles to perfect her craft. After a 2-year hiatus of building and perfecting her sound in LA, she is now ready to showcase a new phase of her music career, known as Koko Røse.
