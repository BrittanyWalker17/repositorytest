WHO ARE WE

____________________



Countryside Photo serves the following communities with great professional photography Stillwater MN , Lake Elmo MN, Bayport MN, Afton MN, Oakdale MN, Mahtomedi MN, Woodbury MN, North St. Paul Minnesota and western Wisconsin .We offer indoor classical studio portraits, outdoor portraits, in home or on location. We are not limited to the classical studio backdrops nor are we limited to only the casual outdoor portraits. We discuss your needs and provide creative images for high school seniors, portraits, families, babies, pets and brides. Our commercial product offerings include product photography, executive portraits, building structures and interiors layout. The digital images we capture are capable of wall sized posters for banners or product booths at exhibits.
